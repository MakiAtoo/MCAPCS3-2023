Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is an anime about curses and cursed energy. The main character: Yuji Itadori, eats a cursed finger of an ancient king. He then was sentenced to death after he eats all 20 fingers. Right now, while waiting for his death, he wants to help people and make them realize tha value of life
